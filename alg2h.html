Class Syllabus: See here for class rules and syllabus.

Course content: We study the fundamental concepts of algebra, with an emphasis on theoretical and computational methods for problem solving. We use geometry and arithmetic to observe patterns and begin practicing writing mathematical proofs, including mathematical induction. Topics will include studying the number systems, exponents, algebraic rules and expressions, functions & their graphs, polynomials, exponential and logarithmic functions, basic linear algebra, sequences and series, analytic geometry, and trigonometry.

Main text: Pearson Algebra 2 Common Core, 2nd Edition.

Other sources: Glencoe, Advanced Mathematical Concepts, PreCalculus with Applications
P. Urban, Da Martin, R Haese, S Haese, Mathematics HL (Core), 2nd Ed.
I. M. Gelfand, A. Shen, Algebra
