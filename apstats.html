Course content: AP Statistics is an introduction to statistics course that introduces students to different concepts and tools used for collecting, analysing, and drawing statistical conclusions from data. We will follow the curriculum set by the CollegeBoard and practice with technology (preferably TI-calculators), problem solving methods, investigations, and technical writiing will be emphasised.

Main text: The Practice of Statistics, 5th Edition (or newer).
