PreCalculus

2021-2022


Instructor: Young Kim (call me "Young")
Classroom: Science Research Center 103
E-mail: ykim@valleychristianschools.org 
Classes: Period 5

Course Content: We study the fundamental concepts of algebra, with an emphasis on theoretical and computational methods for problem solving. We use geometry and arithmetic to observe patterns and begin practicing writing mathematical proofs, including mathematical induction. Topics will include studying the number systems, exponents, algebraic rules and expressions, functions & their graphs, polynomials, exponential and logarithmic functions, basic linear algebra, sequences and series, analytic geometry, and trigonometry. We apply our studies to begin defining the derivative in terms of tangent vectors.

Main text: Glencoe, Advanced Mathematical Concepts, PreCalculus with Applications

Other sources: P. Urban, Da Martin, R Haese, S Haese, Mathematics HL (Core), 2nd Ed.
I. M. Gelfand, A. Shen, Algebra
