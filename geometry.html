Course content: We will discuss logic, proofs, and study how to write a good mathematical proof. We then study geometric constructions (lines, angles, triangles, polygons, circles) and use basic algebra and geometric arguments to prove observations we make. In the second semester, we extend our study to higher dimensions, geometric transformations, basic analytic geometry, and trigonometry.

Main text: R. Larson, L. Boswell,, Geometry, Big Ideas Learning.
Other nice sources: R. Rusczyk, Introduction to Geometry, the Art of Problem Solving.
